# Legal Compliance Playbook - Adeu.ai

This playbook defines the acceptable legal standards for contracts entered into by Adeu.ai. The agent should evaluate incoming agreements against these policies and generate structured changes (redlines) for any deviations.

## Policies

### 1. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

- **Standard:** This Agreement must be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of law principles.
- **Jurisdiction:** The exclusive jurisdiction and venue must be the state or federal courts located in Wilmington, Delaware.
- **Instruction:** If the agreement specifies another state (like California), redline it to Delaware / Wilmington, Delaware.

### 2. Limitation of Liability (Liability Cap)

- **Standard:** The liability cap for either party must not be less than the total fees paid or payable under the Agreement in the twelve (12) months preceding the incident.
- **Instruction:** If the agreement limits liability to less than 12 months (such as 3 months or some other smaller period), redline the limitation cap to twelve (12) months and add a comment bubble explaining the policy requirement.
